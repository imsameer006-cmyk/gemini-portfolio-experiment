# Visual QA Checklist: Senior UX/Product Design Portfolio

This checklist ensures the portfolio meets the high standards expected for Senior Product Design roles, particularly within Enterprise and B2B contexts.

## 1. Spacing & Consistency
- [ ] **Grid Alignment:** Are all primary content blocks aligned to the defined grid (900px content width)?
- [ ] **Section Rhythm:** Is vertical spacing between major sections consistent (`py-24` to `py-36`)?
- [ ] **Internal Padding:** Do cards, callouts, and meta-grids have uniform internal padding (`p-5` to `p-7`)?
- [ ] **Whitespace:** Is there enough "breathing room" to prevent information overload in complex data sections?

## 2. Typography Hierarchy
- [ ] **Clarity of Purpose:** Is Instrument Serif used exclusively for storytelling/narrative, and Geist Sans for functional UI?
- [ ] **Contrast:** Is there a clear visual distinction between labels (`text-xs`), body text (`text-base`), and subheadings (`text-lg`)?
- [ ] **Legibility:** Are paragraph line-lengths optimized for readability (max-width `640px`)?
- [ ] **Consistency:** Are font-weights and tracking consistent for all micro-labels and UI components?

## 3. Visual Polish & Credibility
- [ ] **Enterprise Aesthetic:** Does the UI feel professional and "clean" (neutral palette with high-intent accents)?
- [ ] **Interactive Feedback:** Do all buttons and links have subtle, consistent hover/active states?
- [ ] **Border Quality:** Are borders subtle (`#E6E3DD`) and used purposefully to define structure rather than clutter?
- [ ] **Shadow Elevation:** Are shadows used sparingly and only to indicate depth or interaction (e.g., card hover)?

## 4. Storytelling & Logic
- [ ] **Clarity of Problem:** Is the "Challenge" section visually distinct and easy to scan?
- [ ] **Workflow Logic:** Does the "Short Flow" lifecycle make sense visually? Are stages logically connected?
- [ ] **Role Clarity:** Are role-based interactions (FAE, PAE, etc.) clearly labeled and explained?
- [ ] **Outcome Validation:** Does the "Before/After" section provide a clear, quantifiable comparison?

## 5. Responsive Behavior
- [ ] **Mobile Layout:** Does the content reflow gracefully on small screens? (Check `Stages` vertical stack).
- [ ] **Touch Targets:** Are all interactive elements at least 44px in height/width?
- [ ] **Image Scaling:** Do case study images and videos scale appropriately without losing detail or cropping?
- [ ] **Navigation:** Is the `JumpToNav` accessible and functional on both mobile and desktop?

## 6. Accessibility & Readiness
- [ ] **Alt Text:** Do all images and videos have descriptive `alt` attributes or captions?
- [ ] **Color Contrast:** Does text meet WCAG AA standards against its background?
- [ ] **Reduced Motion:** Does the site respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings (checking `Stages` shimmer and card bloom)?
- [ ] **Asset Readiness:** Are all screenshots and videos high-resolution, cropped correctly, and optimized for web?
